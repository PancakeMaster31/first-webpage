Mafia (a social game)

Mafia is a fun game to play with a large group of people. It can be played anywhere and with anyone.

How to Play:

It is best to play with a group of 7 people and up. There should also be one narrator, who will administer the game. The game will start with the night. And once the night is over the day starts.

Night:

During the night the mafia are the first to wake up and choose someone to kill. Then they fall back asleep and the detective will choose to invsetigate someone. The narrator should take note of who is each role, and each night let the players do their role.

Day:

During the day everyone disusses who they think is bad and if people want to share their role they can. After talking for a little everyone decides two people to put up on trial, and then the people who are accused must state their alibi. Once this is done, everyone votes for who they want to die, and the person with the most votes dies. As they leave the circle, the narrator says their role.

Roles:

Mafia:

  • Mafia (x2) - part of the mafia, who wants to kill all the townies. Chooses someone each night to kill.
  • Godfather - part of the mafia, but is immune to any attacks and comes up as innocent to the detective.
  • Wild Child - starts out as part of the town, but once one mafia dies, they join them.

Townies:

  • Guardian Angel - saves one person each night, which prevents them from dying. Can only save themselves once.
  • Detective - can investigate one person per night, and the narrator gives a thumbs up or down if the person is mafia or not.
  • Hunter - investigates one person per night, and the narrator gives them a thumbs up or down if the person is werewolf or not.
  • Wild Child - starts out as part of the town, but once one mafia dies, they join them.
  • Peeker - during the night, they look to see who the mafia is, but if caught by the mafia they die immediately.
